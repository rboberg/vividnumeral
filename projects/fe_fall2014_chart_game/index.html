Chart Game

You will be shown a series of ten historical price charts for different stocks from different past time periods. For each chart, you will be asked to forecast whether the stock will go up, down, or sideways in the future and how sure you are about your guess. After completing all the charts you can see how all of your predictions actually panned out. The price level and dates have been altered to make the charts anonymous, but each pattern happened in real life.
